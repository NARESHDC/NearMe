Rayakottai

Rayakottai is a historic town in Krishnagiri District, Tamil Nadu. It is well‑known for:

  • The majestic Rayakottai Fort, an 18th‑century hill‑fort once held by Tipu Sultan.
  • Panoramic views from the fort summit, rewarding trekkers with vistas of rocky hills and valleys.
  • Proximity to tourist hubs like Krishnagiri Dam and Hogenakkal Falls.
  • Rich biodiversity, with rugged terrain ideal for short hikes, bird‑watching, and photography.
