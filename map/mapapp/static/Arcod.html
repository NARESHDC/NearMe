Arcod

Arcod is a tranquil village situated in Krishnagiri District, Tamil Nadu. It is well‑known for:

  • Historic temples such as the Sri Venkatesa Perumal Temple and ancient Shiva shrines.
  • Scenic countryside with rolling hills of the Eastern Ghats.
  • Easy access to Krishnagiri Dam, Rayakottai Fort, and other nearby attractions.
  • Serene rural ambience—ideal for bird‑watching, nature walks, and photography.
